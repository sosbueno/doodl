Common Issues

  • Can't connect wallet: Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome recommended) and that Privy is properly loaded.
  • Game not loading: Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache.
  • Prizes not showing: Make sure your wallet is connected and check the blockchain for transaction status.
  • Anti-cheat warning: Keep your browser console closed while playing. If you see a warning, refresh the page.
